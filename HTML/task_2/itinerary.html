My Travel Itinerary

Day Destination Activities Notes
1 Paris, France Visit the Eiffel Tower, explore the Louvre Museum. Book tickets in advance for the Eiffel Tower.
2 Tokyo, Japan Explore Shibuya Crossing, visit Meiji Shrine. Take a walk around Harajuku for street fashion.
3-4 Cape Town, South Africa Visit Table Mountain, explore Robben Island. Weather may be unpredictable; bring warm clothes.
Hermanus (whale watching) Take a boat tour for whale watching. Best to visit between June and November.
